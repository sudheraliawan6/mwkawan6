RULES AND PRINCIPLES

Team member must be Pakistani citizen & he must follow the actions, plans & principles of the Organization.
Once you join the team, you will not discuss politics issues in the events as will as in the general meetings of organiztion.
Every member can give suggestion for he betterment, improvement & planning, but on majority is authority based decision will be taken. So if a member suggestion is not accepted he may not be disappointed.
Each every month meeting will be call, so members should must attend it. If a member not attended more than two times he will be fine.
On event Day everyone follow the order by the team organizer. Everyone will be ready for taking task, & once we give a task he will follow it till finish that event.
For new joining members registeration fee Rs 100 must be submitted along his Form with recent passport size photo.
Those members whose are still students will pay Rs 100 monthly basis, those who on job will paying Rs 200 while those who have a designation will paying Rs 300 each month.
Designations will be handling for only 2 years, after that a committy will be appointed & then they will taken Election for new cabinet.
Those members who will not support, who will not join us in meeting, who will not paying his monthly contribution till 6 months, organization will cancelled his membership.

Mardan Sports Gala 2014

3rd December (International day for persons with disabilities), As it is our wing so that we celebrated it with them on every 3rd December. We organize sports activities, walk, Race & compaign for them on this day. We raise their voice & their rights to the upper management or we also call them for attending such programs. Every year we launching a program for them, In this program we distribute Free wheel chairs to those disable persons who are Deserving. 

CARE FOR ORPHANS

Mardanwal Khalaq Org Launches a free tour to different cities of pakistan for orphans. WE give them transport, Food, Cash Quiz prizes & Sports events for the childs of these orphanage Sarhadi Yateem khana Shamsi Road, Al Islah centre Sheikh Maltoon & Home Sweet Home Baghdada. We also distibute stationaries, sweaters, Shoes, Cloths & blankets for them every year.

LOVE FOR THE NATION

On 14 August 2015 we celebrated Independece day with the help of our Katlang Youth Cabinet. A large number of katlang citizens co operate & join us in this Rally. People also show their pure love for the country on that day. Tehsil Councelors, Nazim & Market president Convey their messages to the people.
